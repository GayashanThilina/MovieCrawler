Research Project Scope

This section has domain details about our project. Describe the information we have gathered by the literature survey.

The research project aims to address the pressing challenges in AI-Based Movie Content Rating and Recommendation Systems, focusing on enhancing personalized cinematic experiences while balancing accuracy and serendipity in recommendations. The scope encompasses the development of innovative algorithms that fuse collaborative filtering and content-based approaches, leveraging natural language processing and deep learning to accurately capture user emotions in sentiment analysis models. This research will further involve the integration of sentiment analysis with recommendation algorithms to enhance the overall cinematic experience by aligning with both user preferences and emotional responses. Additionally, the project will focus on accommodating diverse demographics and cultures by developing recommendation algorithms that incorporate cultural nuances and language-specific preferences. The ultimate goal is to offer a more inclusive and globally relevant cinematic experience while contributing to the film industry's success through real-time trend monitoring and the integration of socio-cultural factors into recommendationÂ models.
